# Study Plan

## Overview
This study plan helps structure interview preparation across several weeks.

## Suggested Weekly Focus
- Week 1: Java fundamentals and collections
- Week 2: Multithreading, JVM, and design patterns
- Week 3: Spring Core, Boot, Security, and JPA
- Week 4: SQL, transactions, and indexing
- Week 5: Kafka, RabbitMQ, Redis, Hazelcast
- Week 6: React, TypeScript, REST APIs, and Node.js
- Week 7: Docker, AWS, Kubernetes, and system design
- Week 8: Mock interviews and revision
